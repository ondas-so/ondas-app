    Introduction

    Thank you for using ONDAS. This is a free, browser-based screenwriting editor — built by a screenwriter who wanted a tool that stayed out of the way and let him write.     What ONDAS Is

    ONDAS gives you a clean, distraction-free environment to write screenplays in Fountain syntax. It includes features like focus mode, beat board, margin notes, revision colour-coding, and export to PDF, Final Draft (.fdx), Fountain, and plain text. You can use it without an account, entirely in your browser, with nothing leaving your device unless you choose to enable cloud sync. We provide documentation and updates through the editor itself and, for those who opt in, by email.
